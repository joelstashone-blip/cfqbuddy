# THE RED SEAL ACADEMY - PRIVACY POLICY **Effective Date:** June 5, 2026 **Company:** THE RED SEAL ACADEMY **Website:** cfqbuddy.ca (transitioning to TheRedSealAcademy.com) --- ## 1. INTRODUCTION THE RED SEAL ACADEMY ("we," "our," "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services. **Key Principle:** We collect only the information necessary to provide excellent exam preparation services and never sell your personal data. --- ## 2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT ### 2.1 Registration Information **Personal Account:** - Full name (first and last) - Email address - Phone number - Home address (street, city, province, postal code) - Shipping address (if different from home) **Business Account:** - All personal information above, plus: - Company name - Business registration number - GST/HST number - Industry/trade specialization ### 2.2 Usage Information - Exam performance and scoring data - Time spent on questions and sections - Login frequency and session duration - Device and browser information - IP address and general location ### 2.3 Payment Information - Payment processing handled by Gumroad (third-party) - We receive only transaction confirmation data - No credit card details stored on our systems ### 2.4 Communication Records - Support ticket content and correspondence - Email communications and preferences - Feedback and survey responses --- ## 3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION ### 3.1 Service Delivery - **Account management:** Creating and maintaining your account - **Exam delivery:** Providing access to practice materials - **Progress tracking:** Monitoring your study performance - **Technical support:** Resolving platform issues ### 3.2 Communication - **Service updates:** Changes to exams or platform features - **Educational content:** Study tips and exam preparation guidance - **Marketing communications:** New trade offerings (with your consent) - **Support responses:** Answering questions and providing assistance ### 3.3 Business Operations - **Tax compliance:** Calculating appropriate Canadian taxes - **Performance analytics:** Improving exam quality and platform functionality - **Legal compliance:** Meeting regulatory and legal requirements ### 3.4 Product Development - **Content improvement:** Enhancing exam questions and explanations - **Feature development:** Building better study tools - **Quality assurance:** Ensuring accurate and current materials --- ## 4. INFORMATION SHARING & DISCLOSURE ### 4.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data - **No data brokers:** We never sell personal information to third parties - **No advertising networks:** Your data is not shared with advertisers - **No marketing lists:** We don't provide your information to other companies ### 4.2 Limited Sharing Circumstances **Service Providers:** - Gumroad (payment processing only) - Email service provider (for communications you've consented to) - Web hosting and security services **Legal Requirements:** - Court orders or legal subpoenas - Government agency requests (with proper authorization) - Protection of our rights or safety of users ### 4.3 Business Transfers - Information may transfer if business is sold or merged - Users notified of any such changes - Same privacy protections maintained --- ## 5.
